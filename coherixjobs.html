Careers at Coherix
Coherix, Inc. is the leading provider of 3D machine vision solutions for manufacturing environments with complex measurement challenges – where measuring in microns within microseconds is mission critical. We appreciate your interest in Coherix and encourage you to explore current opportunities. We are growing rapidly and are always seeking passionate and talented people to join our enthusiastic and nimble team. If you are a dynamic individual that enjoys a fast-paced environment, you have come to the right place. Coherix is an equal opportunity employer. We are located in Ann Arbor, Michigan with global opportunities for individuals seeking a high-tech, creative environment and an incentive stock option program. We offer a competitive salary and benefits plan. Success at Coherix is based on experience, performance and contribution within The Coherix Way.

Project Engineer
Coordinate the planning, design, and installation of machinery and equipment in an industrial environment using atherectomy actuator, phantom box. Oversee installation to see that machines and equipment are installed and functioning according to specifications. Test the machinery to make sure that machinery has the capability to carry out desired functions. Coordinate with management to ensure that proper training is provided to workers to implement operating procedures, safety protocols to avoid system malfunctions. Participate in training sessions, seminars, and continuing education to stay abreast of new technical developments. Provide technical information and training to workers in other departments, such as marketing and legal, as well as engineering and production staff.
